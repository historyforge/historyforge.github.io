  • The History Center in Tompkins County is the founder and lead partner of HistoryForge. HistoryForge originally focused on Ithaca in the 1910 US Census. It has since expanded to have records from all US census and all of Tompkins County.

    Visit Tompkins NY HistoryForge

  • The Chemung County Historical Society was one of HistoryForge's first three partners. It has over 28,000 records from Elmira's 1910 US Census.

    Visit Elmira NY HistoryForge

  • The Local History Discovery Center of Seymour Public Library in Auburn NY was one of HistoryForge's first three partners. It has over 28,000 records from Auburn's 1910 US Census.

    Visit Auburn NY HistoryForge

  • The Schenectady County Historical Society has been entering their census records for the 1910 US Census.

    Visit Schenectady NY HistoryForge

  • The Walnut Hills Historical Society in Cincinnati preserves the history of a diverse neighborhood that overlooks Cincinnati and the Ohio River. Their HistoryForge coverage spans the 1920, 1930, and 1940 US Censuses.

    Visit Walnut Hills OH HistoryForge

  • The Heinz History Center of Pittsburgh PA has been piloting HistoryForge, with a focus on the 1920 US Census.

    Visit Pittsburgh PA HistoryForge
